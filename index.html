About

Cybersecurity Specialist & Vulnerability Researcher.

Cybersecurity student at the University of Cincinnati with a focus on offensive security and vulnerability research. Experienced in assessing enterprise environments through internal network testing and Active Directory security assessments.

I conduct independent security research and participate in vulnerability disclosure and bug bounty programs, where I identify vulnerabilities in binaries, web applications, APIs, and production systems. I am passionate about uncovering security weaknesses, understanding and demonstrating their impact, and delivering clear, actionable findings that help organizations strengthen their security posture.

My interests include offensive security, red team operations, adversary emulation, threat hunting, and vulnerability research. I have been credited with multiple CVEs and continue to pursue opportunities to advance the security of real-world systems through responsible disclosure and applied research.
