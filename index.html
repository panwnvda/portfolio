About

Hi, I'm Wencheng Xue.

I'm an offensive security engineer, vulnerability researcher, and red team operator with a focus on building tools, understanding complex systems, and continuously improving my craft. My primary interests include offensive security engineering, adversary simulation, and vulnerability research.

I enjoy developing tools that help operators work more effectively, studying how modern defenses detect malicious activity, and exploring the tradecraft used in real-world operations. Most of my projects focus on C2 development, offensive tooling, automation, and Windows internals. I have contributed vulnerability research to open-source security projects and have a strong interest in both discovering and responsibly disclosing security issues. Beyond finding vulnerabilities, I enjoy understanding why they exist, how they can be exploited, and how they can ultimately be fixed.

This site is where I keep my research, notes, projects, and tools.
